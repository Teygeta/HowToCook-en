## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Tools
  - Microwave oven
  - 2 large bowls (recommended microwave-specific bowls)
  - 1 small bowl
- Raw materials
  - Rice 200 ml
  - Sausage 1
  - Egg 1
  - Carrot 1
  - Salt
  - Oil 15 ml
  - Light soy sauce 10 ml
  - Scallions 1

## Calculation

1 serving.
